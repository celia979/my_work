
imagine a word where your lifespan isn't determined by fate, 0biology or chance but by a single stroke of a brush.


In this game every year an enigmatic entity known as the paintress awakens from her slumber and paints a number onto her monolith a degree that dictates how long humanity may live, so in a desperate attempt to find answers a group of people each just one year away from death set sail across the sea to the place where she resides. However years pass by and not a  single expedition returns, eventually the human life span shrinks to just 33 years.

Then begins your journey as part of expedition 33. As you travel through a lavish world inspired by  19th century European art,


you play as Gustav as he fights his way through in a sleek turn based combat system inspired by games like final fantasy and persona but with a twist instead of selecting a move and then idly waiting for your turn the game mixes real time mechanics that have you perform quick timed actions during a battle to either boost your moves or avoid incoming attacks 


but that's not all, you also be managing a party of several other characters each with distinct class story and skill tree
